# KYC Quick Start Guide

## üöÄ Getting Started with KYC

### For New Customers (2 Minutes)

#### Step 1: Open the App
When you first open FNG, you'll see the KYC registration form automatically.

#### Step 2: Fill Personal Info
- Enter your full name (as on your ID)
- Add your email and phone number
- Select your date of birth and gender
- Click **Next**

#### Step 3: Enter Address
- Type your complete home address
- Enter your city
- Select your state from the dropdown
- Click **Next**

#### Step 4: ID Verification
- Choose your ID type (NIN, Driver's License, etc.)
- Enter your ID number
- Enter your 11-digit BVN
- Click **Next**

#### Step 5: Upload Documents
- **Upload ID**: Take a clear photo of your ID (front & back)
- **Upload Proof of Address**: Recent utility bill or bank statement
- **Upload Selfie**: Take a selfie holding your ID
- Click **Submit Application**

#### Step 6: Wait for Approval
- You'll see "Application Submitted" message
- Orange banner shows "Under Review"
- Admin will review within 24-48 hours
- You'll be notified when approved

---

### For Administrators (30 Seconds)

#### To Review Applications:

1. **Switch to Admin Mode** (button in header)

2. **Go to Customers** (click icon in sidebar)

3. **See Pending Applications** (orange badge shows count)

4. **Review Each Application:**
   - Click "Review Details"
   - Check all 4 tabs (Personal, Address, Verification, Documents)
   - Verify documents are clear and valid

5. **Make Decision:**
   - Click **"Approve"** if everything is valid ‚úÖ
   - Click **"Reject"** if documents are invalid ‚ùå

6. **Done!** Customer is notified and can access the app.

## üìã Checklist for Customers

Before starting KYC, make sure you have:

- [ ] Valid government-issued ID (NIN, Driver's License, Voter's Card, or Passport)
- [ ] Your 11-digit BVN (Bank Verification Number)
- [ ] Recent utility bill or bank statement (not older than 3 months)
- [ ] Smartphone or camera to take photos
- [ ] Your email address and phone number
- [ ] 5-10 minutes of time

## üìã Checklist for Admins

When reviewing applications, verify:

- [ ] Name matches ID document
- [ ] Email format is valid
- [ ] Phone number is Nigerian format
- [ ] Date of birth makes customer 18+ years old
- [ ] Address is complete and valid
- [ ] ID type is selected
- [ ] ID number matches document
- [ ] BVN is 11 digits
- [ ] ID document is clear and not expired
- [ ] Proof of address is recent (< 3 months)
- [ ] Selfie shows customer holding ID
- [ ] No signs of fraud or fake documents

## ‚ö° Quick Tips

### For Customers:

**üì∏ Taking Good Photos:**
- Use good lighting (natural light is best)
- Hold camera steady
- Make sure text is readable
- No glare or shadows
- Take multiple photos and choose the best

**‚úÖ BVN Tips:**
- It's always 11 digits
- Dial `*565*0#` on your phone to get it
- Keep it secure and private
- Required by Nigerian banking regulations

**üìÑ Proof of Address:**
Best documents to use:
- Electricity bill
- Water bill
- Bank statement
- Phone bill
- Government letter

Must be dated within last 3 months!

### For Admins:

**üîç Red Flags to Watch:**
- Blurry or unclear photos
- Expired ID documents
- Address proof older than 3 months
- Mismatched names
- Suspicious selfie (different person)
- Invalid email format
- Wrong phone number format

**‚úÖ Quick Approval:**
If everything looks good:
1. Quick scan all 4 tabs (15 seconds)
2. Click Approve (2 seconds)
3. Move to next application

**‚ùå When to Reject:**
- Documents are unreadable
- ID is expired
- Selfie doesn't match ID
- BVN format is wrong
- Obvious fraud attempt
- Missing required documents

## ‚ùì Quick FAQ

### Customer Questions:

**Q: How long does KYC take?**
A: Form takes 5-10 minutes. Approval within 24-48 hours.

**Q: What if I don't have my BVN?**
A: Dial `*565*0#` on any phone linked to your bank account.

**Q: Can I use the app while waiting?**
A: Yes, but with limited access. Full access after approval.

**Q: What if I'm rejected?**
A: Contact support or resubmit with better documents.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and secure. We comply with NDPR.

### Admin Questions:

**Q: How many can I approve per day?**
A: As many as you need. Each takes ~30 seconds to review.

**Q: What if I'm not sure about an application?**
A: Reject it and ask customer to contact support.

**Q: Can I undo an approval/rejection?**
A: Not currently. Future versions will support this.

**Q: Where do documents go?**
A: Currently in localStorage. Production will use secure cloud storage.

**Q: How do I know if a document is fake?**
A: Look for: poor quality, mismatched info, signs of editing. When in doubt, reject.
