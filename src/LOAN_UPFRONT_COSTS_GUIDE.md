## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Why do I need to pay upfront costs?
**A**: Upfront costs protect both you and FNG:
- **Deposit**: Shows commitment and is refunded
- **Insurance**: Protects against default risk
- **Service Charge**: Covers processing costs

### Q2: Is the deposit really refunded?
**A**: Yes! 100% of the deposit is refunded after full repayment. It's tracked in the system and automatically processed.

### Q3: Can I get a loan without paying upfront?
**A**: No. Upfront payment is mandatory for all loans. This is a standard practice in microfinance.

### Q4: What if I can't afford the upfront cost?
**A**: Consider:
- Applying for a smaller loan amount
- Using your contribution balance (if sufficient)
- Saving up before applying
- Borrowing from family for the upfront

### Q5: Do I pay upfront before or after approval?
**A**: After approval. The flow is:
1. Apply â†’ 2. Get approved â†’ 3. Pay upfront â†’ 4. Receive loan

### Q6: What happens if I default on the loan?
**A**: Your deposit is used to cover the outstanding balance. You won't get it refunded.

### Q7: Can I pay upfront in installments?
**A**: No. Upfront costs must be paid in full before loan disbursement.

### Q8: Is insurance refundable?
**A**: No. Insurance and service charge are non-refundable fees.

### Q9: Why is insurance higher for Jumbo loans?
**A**: Larger loans carry higher risk, so insurance rates increase accordingly.

### Q10: When exactly do I get my deposit back?
**A**: Within 72 hours of making your final loan payment, provided all conditions are met.
